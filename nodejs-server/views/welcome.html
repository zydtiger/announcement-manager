The beta version user guides

Account Binding

Binds your accounts in the top right btn.

Left

The left side integrates the announcements tabs in which you can click into to get notifications from the corresponding website. You will have to bind your accounts before doing so. The first time you go in, you may see nothing. You should wait until the initialization is finished. Whenever you want to get the latest notifications, press 'Sync'

Top

The top bar integrates two functions only. One for changing account settings and one for logging out, respectively.
